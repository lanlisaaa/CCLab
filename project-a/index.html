[ Melragia Neurotia ]

Scientific Name:

Melragia Neurotia

Discovered by / at:

Lan at 12am July 17 while observing an actor standing in front of a mirror having self-doubts

Family:

Interneurons

Habitat:

Found most commonly in human brain when the individual is under pressure and experience strong negative emotions

Appearance:

A round cell with a nucleus that changes color as it goes through different emotions. She sends magenta electric signals to all parts of the cell and spits purple teeth for self-defense purposes when pressure is applied.

Key Characteristics:

  • She calls out for help every time she detects that the individual is under pressure.
  • If her alert is left unattended, her help signals would form a huge net to trap her.
  • She sends magenta electric signals to all parts of the cell and spits purple spikes for self-defense purposes when pressure is applied.
  • When she changes color depending on her mood. She turns red when she is enraged. She turns blue. If she’s experiencing a mixed feelings of these two emotions, the colors would combine itself.

What we know so far about [Melragia Neurotia]? The purpose of her existence is to remind people to take care of their mental health. She appears when individuals are under pressure or experience a traumatic event, and hates being ignored. Many people’s first reaction is to repress her every time she appears. However, the more you try to repress her the more frantic she gets.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature You can change the color of Melragia by moving your mouse. If you want to press down the feeling of grief or anger, you can press your mouse.
